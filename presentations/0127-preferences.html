The PPT Model of Preferences: Essential Elements

Essential Elements
  • A set of alternatives
  • One of the following binary comparisons for each unique pair of alternatives $(x,y)$:
    • The person prefers $x$ to $y$
    • The person prefers $y$ to $x$
    • The person is indifferent between $x$ and $y$
